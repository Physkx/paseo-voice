## [0.4.0] - 2026-07-21

- **Voice routing**: Add named OpenAI, xAI, and compatible voice profiles with idle-only switching
- **Dictation**: Add independent cleanup profiles with connection-scoped selection and no failover
- **Configuration**: Replace legacy OpenAI and Spark fields with typed profiles and named credentials
- **Browser protocol**: Add protocol-v3 profile catalogues, selectors, and generation-safe reset state
- **xAI authentication**: Support refreshed Grok subscription OAuth on exact official xAI routes
